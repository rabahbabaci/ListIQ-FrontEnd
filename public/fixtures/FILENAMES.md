# Demo Photo Filenames

To use the photo upload demo, save images with these exact filenames
(any image extension works: .jpg, .jpeg, .png, .webp):

- levis-denim-jacket.jpg
- nike-sneakers.jpg
- jordan-sneakers.jpg
- louis-vuitton-handbag.jpg
- coach-crossbody-bag.jpg
- anthropologie-midi-dress.jpg
- harley-davidson-vintage-t-shirt.jpg
- levis-leather-jacket.jpg
- handm-midi-dress.jpg
- unknown-blazer.jpg
- unknown-midi-dress.jpg
- zara-denim-jacket.jpg
- old-navy-denim-jacket.jpg
- unknown-denim-jacket.jpg
- coach-handbag.jpg
- free-people-midi-dress.jpg

When you upload a file with one of these names, the app loads the matching
fixture and displays your uploaded photo throughout the flow.

If the filename doesn't match, the app falls back to showing the Levi's
denim jacket analysis with your uploaded photo.
